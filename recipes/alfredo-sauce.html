Alfredo Sauce

Once you try this homemade Alfredo sauce recipe, youâ€™ll never go back to the jarred stuff.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, then stir in the cream and simmer.
  2. Whisk in the cheese and garlic until heated through and smooth.
  3. Stir in the parsley.
